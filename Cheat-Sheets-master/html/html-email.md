### Selectors to avoid

`E[attr]` | (Outlook, Gmail) |  
`E:nth-child(n)` | (Outlook, Gmail) |  
`::before` *and* `::after` | (Outlook, Yahoo,Gmail) |  
`E F` | (Gmail) |  
`E + F`, `E > F` *etc* | (Outlook, Gmail)|

Inline your CSS as much as possible.
