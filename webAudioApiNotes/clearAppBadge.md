Navigator.clearAppBadge()
=========================

The **`clearAppBadge()`** method of the [`Navigator`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Navigator) interface clears a badge on the current app's icon by setting it to `nothing`. The value `nothing` indictes that no badge is currently set, and the status of the badge is *cleared*.

[Syntax](#syntax "Permalink to Syntax")
---------------------------------------

    let promise = Navigator.clearAppBadge();

### [Parameters](#parameters "Permalink to Parameters")

None.

### [Return value](#returns "Permalink to Return value")

A [`Promise`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Promise) that resolves with [`undefined`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/undefined).

### [Exceptions](#exceptions "Permalink to Exceptions")

`NotSupportedError`  
The [`Navigator`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Navigator) does not have a document that this action can be acted on.

[Examples](#examples "Permalink to Examples")
---------------------------------------------

Once all messages in an application have been read, call `clearAppBadge()` to clear the badge and remove the notification.

    navigator.clearAppBadge()
